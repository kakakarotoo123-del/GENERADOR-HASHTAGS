Do YouTube hashtags help you get more views?

Yes, but YouTube hashtags work differently from other platforms. YouTube shows the first 3 hashtags you add to your description directly above your video title as clickable links — this places your video inside topic-based playlists that YouTube actively promotes.

YouTube recommends using no more than 15 hashtags. Using more than 15 can result in YouTube ignoring all your hashtags entirely, so quality over quantity is essential here.

The most impactful strategy is to use a broad hashtag (#tutorial), a niche one (#webdevelopment), and your brand name (#yourchannelname) — this combination maximizes both discoverability and brand building.
