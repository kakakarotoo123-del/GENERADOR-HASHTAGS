Do TikTok hashtags actually work?

Yes — but not the way most people use them. Flooding your caption with 20+ random hashtags does not help on TikTok. The algorithm is smarter than that.

The best strategy is to use 3–5 relevant hashtags: one or two broad ones (#fyp, #viral) combined with 2–3 niche-specific ones that tell the algorithm exactly who your audience is. This precision gets your video shown to the right people faster.

TikTok also recommends adding hashtags in your video caption, not in the comments — unlike Instagram, where comment hashtags also work.
