How to use Instagram hashtags effectively

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. While you can use all 30, studies suggest that 10–20 well-targeted hashtags tend to perform better than maxing out with generic ones.

The key is using a mix of hashtag sizes: combine popular hashtags (over 1 million posts) with medium ones (100K–500K) and niche-specific ones (under 100K). This mix gives your content a chance to rank in smaller categories while also appearing in larger feeds.

Place your hashtags either at the end of your caption or in the first comment of your post — both strategies work equally well according to Instagram's algorithm.
