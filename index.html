Frequently Asked Questions

How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Using 10–20 targeted hashtags tends to perform better than maxing out with irrelevant ones.

Do hashtags work on TikTok?

Yes. TikTok hashtags help the algorithm understand your content and show it to the right audience. Use 3–5 niche-specific hashtags for best results.

Are hashtags useful on LinkedIn?

Absolutely. LinkedIn hashtags increase the discoverability of your posts among professionals in your industry. Use 3–5 relevant hashtags per post.

Do YouTube hashtags help with SEO?

Yes. YouTube shows up to 3 hashtags above your video title, and they appear as clickable links that help users find related content.
