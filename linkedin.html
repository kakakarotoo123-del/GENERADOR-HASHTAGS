How many hashtags should you use on LinkedIn?

LinkedIn recommends using 3–5 hashtags per post. Unlike Instagram, using too many hashtags on LinkedIn can look spammy and actually reduce your organic reach.

The best approach is to use 1 broad hashtag (#marketing), 1–2 industry-specific ones (#digitalmarketing), and 1 niche or trending hashtag relevant to your specific post. This combination maximizes visibility across different audiences.

Place hashtags at the end of your post for a clean, professional look — LinkedIn users expect this format and it doesn't disrupt the reading experience.
